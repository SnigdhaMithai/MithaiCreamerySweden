About Us

Mithai Creamery is a small-batch artisanal ice cream brand based in Stockholm, blending traditional Indian flavors with modern European techniques. Our flavors are stories — made with love, memory, and celebration.

Signature Flavors

Nolen Gur Ice Cream

Rich date palm jaggery blended into a creamy base, topped with edible gold flakes.

Coconut & Mango

Silky coconut cream ice cream topped with Alphonso mango sorbet.

Toasted Gobindo Bhog

Caramelized puffed rice and toasted rice base with Crème Dulce.

Gondhoraj Lebu Sorbet

Refreshing citrus sorbet with caramelized zest and raspberry compote swirl.
